About BitAgric

Empowering the future of farming through technology, knowledge, and insight.

Our Mission

BitAgric exists to democratize agricultural knowledge and tools for farmers of all scales. We combine cutting-edge technology with practical farming wisdom to create accessible solutions that enhance productivity, sustainability, and profitability in agriculture.

Our platform bridges the gap between traditional farming knowledge and modern digital solutions, helping farmers make data-driven decisions while respecting time-tested agricultural practices.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every farmer, regardless of location or scale, has access to the tools and knowledge needed to optimize their operations sustainably. By 2030, we aim to be the leading digital companion for 1 million farmers worldwide, helping increase yields while reducing environmental impact through smart farming practices.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Innovation

We continuously develop practical solutions that address real farming challenges through technology.

Sustainability

We promote farming methods that protect the environment while ensuring economic viability.

Knowledge Sharing

We believe agricultural wisdom should be freely accessible to all who need it.

Inclusivity

Our solutions are designed for farmers of all scales, from smallholders to commercial operations.

Empowerment

We equip farmers with tools and insights to take control of their agricultural outcomes.

Transparency

We maintain open communication about our methods, data sources, and business practices.

Our Journey

Milestones in our growth and development

2018

Foundation

BitAgric was founded by agricultural engineers and data scientists who saw the potential for technology to transform small-scale farming in East Africa.

2019

First Prototype

Launched our initial budgeting tool after field testing with 50 smallholder farmers in Kenya.

2020

Platform Expansion

Added crop encyclopedia and marketing features based on user feedback, reaching 5,000 active users.

2022

Regional Growth

Expanded services to Tanzania and Uganda, forming partnerships with agricultural cooperatives.

2023

Mobile Integration

Launched SMS-based tools to serve farmers without smartphones, doubling our user base.

Jamal Mwangi

Founder & CEO

Agricultural engineer with 15 years experience in smallholder farm optimization across East Africa. Jamal founded BitAgric after seeing firsthand how technology could transform yields for small-scale farmers.

Prior to BitAgric, Jamal worked with the Kenyan Ministry of Agriculture developing irrigation solutions for arid regions. He holds a PhD in Agricultural Engineering from the University of Nairobi.

Aisha Okoth

Lead Agronomist

Specializes in tropical crop systems and sustainable soil management practices. Aisha brings 12 years of field experience working with smallholder farmers across East Africa.

She leads our agronomy research team and ensures all crop recommendations are scientifically validated and locally appropriate. Aisha holds a Masters in Tropical Agriculture from Makerere University.
